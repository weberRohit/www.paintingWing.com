Thinks to keep in mind during your Screening

1. Your introduction should follow the same order as your name, branch, batch, place of residence, matriculation, higher secondary, hobbies, strengths, weaknesses, and aim.

2. Never use abbreviations like GATE, GRE, ISRO, etc, instead use the full form.

3. If you are confused with your strengths and weaknesses part then google the examples of it to take the reference.

4. People with the hobby as sketching/drawing/painting should be ready at the moment to show their artworks during Screening (if asked)

5. If possible try to be in your casuals (light color plane shirt)

6. Must go through all the below details about Painting Wing.

7. Lastly, assuming that this will be your first Screening, and anything we do for the first time is always special, so don’t be nervous, be confident and give your best.

Painting Wing BIT Sindri

1.The club's mission is to nurture and cultivate an arts culture among students on campus. Every year, we aim to create a vibrant and colorful atmosphere on campus through our unique and immersive activities.

2. The tagline of the club is Let Satisfaction prevail.

3.The painting wing is one such platform that promotes creativity. Our work reflects how creative we are! It is the college's only fine-arts group and carries the torch for all things ART. The Painting Wing is open to anybody who want to develop his or her creative abilities, not simply artists.

OFFLINE EVENTS

1.Samarpan:The centrepiece event Samarpan is our yearly major event that allows young talented students from our campus and beyond to demonstrate their artistic abilities. It is an attractive two-day art expo during which numerous art competitions are organised, such as the face painting competition, wall painting competition, and many more.

2.Creations:This is our yearly inter-school event. All local schools outside our college, including government schools, participate in this event, which provides them with a platform to compete against students from other schools. We also provide free painting and sketching lessons for students who are unable to afford professional classes.

3.Akriti:Akriti is an exciting event organized exclusively for girls of the first year. The event comprises various sections like
● Best Dress
● Card and Craft making
● 1min Act
● Rangoli and a few other events.

4.Graffiti:In near words means drawings that are done on the wall or on other surface in a public space. Every year we choose a theme for the event, last year it was 'Platinum Jublee'. Painting is done on the wall of admission building of Bit Sindri. Students from each year participate in this to paint the walls of the admission building and enhance the beauty of BIT Sindri

ONLINE EVENTS

1.Faceoff:It is an important online event for the painting wing. This competition is primarily for our newcomers. The goal of this event is simply to introduce freshers to their batchmates from various departments of BIT in a fun way. The most influential person is recognised and wins a prize at the end.

2.Shades:Every year we conduct a national event Shades, which is an online drawing & painting competition, where participants submit their creations to the Painting Wing. This competition is a one-month-long event and is open to anyone from any college or school. This competition basically comprises of 5 divisions
1. Sketching
2. Painting
3. Digital art
4. Doodling
5. Grafitti

3.INSIGNIA:A logo-designing competition in which contestants have to redesign and recreate a logo from the pool of companies provided
